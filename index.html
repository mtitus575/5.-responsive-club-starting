Civilian drone flying

Civilian drone flying has grown significantly, transforming industries and hobbies. Drones are used for various purposes, including aerial photography, surveying, wildlife monitoring, and delivery. Advances in technology have made them more affordable and accessible, appealing to both hobbyists and professionals. However, their increasing use raises concerns about safety, privacy, and regulation, prompting many countries to implement guidelines to ensure responsible operation while encouraging innovation.
